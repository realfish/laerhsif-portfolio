Final

As a souvenir of CNPolitics, this tote bag presents the humorous Chinese slogan 靠谱、温柔、深入、持久地谈论中国 which literally means “talking about China reasonably, gently, deeply and persistently”.

There were two proposals of the doodle design, one to parody the Ivy League emblems and the other to parody the Standard Dictionary of Contemporary Chinese 现代汉语规范词典. In the year 2016, the latter design went into production through the cooperation with a Chi Heng Foundation project for AIDS refief.
